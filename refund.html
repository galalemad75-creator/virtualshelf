Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: April 4, 2026

1. Overview

At VirtualShelf, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchases. This Refund and Return Policy outlines the conditions and procedures for returning products and receiving refunds for orders placed through our platform.

2. Return Eligibility

You may request a return for the following reasons:

  • Damaged Products: Items that arrived damaged or broken
  • Wrong Items: Products different from what was ordered
  • Expired Products: Items past their expiration date at time of delivery
  • Quality Issues: Products that do not meet quality standards
  • Missing Items: Products listed in your order but not received

3. Return Timeframe

  • Perishable goods: Must be reported within 24 hours of delivery
  • Non-perishable goods: Must be reported within 7 days of delivery
  • Damaged items: Must be reported within 48 hours with photographic evidence

Requests submitted after these timeframes may not be eligible for a refund.

4. How to Request a Return

  1. Navigate to your order history in the app
  2. Select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Click "Request Return" and select the reason
  4. Upload photos if applicable (for damaged/quality issues)
  5. Submit your request

Alternatively, you can contact us directly at emadh5156@gmail.com or through our Contact Us page.

5. Refund Process

Once your return request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days
  • The refund will be credited to the original payment method
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund is processed
  • For store credit refunds, the credit will be applied immediately to your account

6. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return or refund:

  • Items that have been consumed or used beyond reasonable inspection
  • Products without proof of purchase
  • Items purchased during final clearance sales (unless defective)
  • Gift cards and vouchers

7. Partial Refunds

In certain situations, only partial refunds may be granted:

  • Items returned in damaged condition not due to our error
  • Items returned after the standard return timeframe but within 30 days
  • Orders with multiple items where only some qualify for return

8. Exchanges

If you received a defective or damaged item, we can offer an exchange for the same product. If you prefer a different product, please return the original item and place a new order.

9. Cancellation Policy

  • Before processing: Orders can be cancelled free of charge before they enter the warehouse picking process
  • After processing: Orders that have entered the picking process may be cancelled, but a restocking fee may apply
  • After delivery: Please follow the return process outlined above

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund and Return Policy, please contact us:

Email: emadh5156@gmail.com

Through our website: Contact Us page
